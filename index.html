CrystalsCollector!

a Math and Memory Game

At the start of the game, you will be given a Random Number to Match.
There are four crystals below, each representing a different value or points. By clicking on a crystal, you will add the specific amount of points to your accumulated score.
You win the game by matching your accumulated score to the random number, you lose if your total goes above.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
On start, the values of each crystal and the random number changes.
